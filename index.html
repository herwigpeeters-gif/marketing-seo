Content Strategy & Clusters

To build topical authority that satisfies both Google and AI search engines, our content strategy is segmented into three distinct pillars targeting specific executive needs.

🤝

Cluster 1: Leadership & People

Establish Vlerick as the leader in "soft skills" and "human-centric" management.

people management opleiding, leadership training, leading digital transformation
📈

Cluster 2: Career Impact & ROI

Provide hard evidence of the value of the investment to decision-makers.

MBA salary increase, executive networking, alumni success stories
⌚

Cluster 3: Program Flexibility

Address the logistical barriers to entry for busy working professionals.

Online MBA for executives, part-time MBA structure, modular learning

Target Keyword Tiers

  • Executive MBA Belgium: Targets geographical intent for local executives.
  • Triple-Accredited MBA: Targets professionals looking for the "gold standard" in business education.
  • Global MBA for Professionals: Positions the program as an international career accelerator.
  • Executive MBA for working professionals: Targets the need for work-life balance.
  • ROI of MBA for senior managers: Captures those in the final justification stage of the funnel.
  • Part-time MBA for corporate leadership tracks: Specifically targets HR leaders and talent managers.

Because MBA programs are high-cost (YMYL - Your Money Your Life), prioritize:

  • ✔
    Faculty Expertise Links to published research from professors.
  • ✔
    Alumni Evidence In-depth case studies of graduates in C-suite positions.
  • ✔
    Accreditation Visibility Ensure Equis, AMBA, and AACSB logos are prominent.
